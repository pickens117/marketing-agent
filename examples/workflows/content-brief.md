# Content Brief Example

## Objective
Create a blog post that supports launch messaging and captures search demand.

## Audience
B2B marketing leaders and marketing ops practitioners.

## Content angle
How to improve reporting speed without sacrificing trust in the data.

## Key takeaways
Why reporting trust matters, how AI can help, where human review still matters.

## Structure or outline
Problem, stakes, solution approach, practical checklist, CTA.

## Distribution plan
Blog, LinkedIn snippets, email newsletter, repurposed webinar talking points.

## CTA
Download the benchmark checklist.

## Success metrics
Organic traffic, engagement time, CTA clicks, assisted conversions.
