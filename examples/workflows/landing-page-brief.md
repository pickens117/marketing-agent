# Landing Page Brief Example

## Objective
Convert paid and email traffic into demo requests for an AI reporting feature.

## Audience
VP Marketing and marketing ops leaders evaluating reporting workflows.

## Primary message
Get faster reporting clarity without sacrificing analyst control or trust.

## Page structure
Hero, problem framing, solution overview, proof, CTA block, FAQ.

## Proof and trust elements
Customer evidence, benchmark snippet, analyst-control explanation.

## CTA strategy
Primary CTA: Book a demo. Secondary CTA: Download checklist.

## Conversion risks
Weak message match, vague AI claims, insufficient proof.

## Measurement plan
CVR, CTA clicks, scroll depth, form completion rate.
