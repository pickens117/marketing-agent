# AI Adoption Plan Example

## Objective
Improve content and demand gen throughput without lowering quality.

## Current-state assumptions
Small team, limited ops support, moderate AI familiarity.

## Best initial use cases
Prompt improvement, repurposing, briefing, and campaign experimentation.

## Recommended workflow changes
Add AI draft stage, human review stage, and claim check stage.

## Roles and responsibilities
Content lead approves voice, demand gen lead approves campaign logic, ops owns templates.

## Prompting and QA guidance
Use approved message house and require claim verification before publishing.

## Governance and risk controls
No unsupported ROI claims, no customer proof without approval, no sensitive data in prompts.

## Enablement and training plan
Run one kickoff workshop and weekly office hours for the first month.

## Success metrics
Draft cycle time, revision rate, stakeholder satisfaction, campaign throughput.

## 30-day rollout plan
Pilot on one campaign, review quality weekly, expand after two successful cycles.
