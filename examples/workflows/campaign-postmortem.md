# Campaign Postmortem Example

## Objective and scope
Review Q2 launch campaign performance and extract learnings for the next launch.

## Performance summary
Strong CTR and registration volume, weaker than expected opportunity creation.

## What worked
Outcome-led messaging and webinar promotion creative.

## What did not work
Landing page conversion and low-intent retargeting audience.

## Root-cause analysis
Message-to-page mismatch and weak qualification on some channels.

## Recommendations
Improve offer clarity, tighten retargeting, align CTA with buying stage.

## Follow-up experiments
Test demo CTA vs checklist CTA and shorter landing page hero.

## Owner and next steps
Demand gen lead to update landing page and paid audience mix next sprint.
