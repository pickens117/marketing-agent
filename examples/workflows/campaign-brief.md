# Campaign Brief Example

## Objective
Generate qualified pipeline for a new AI reporting feature launch.

## Audience
VP Marketing and Demand Gen leaders at mid-market SaaS companies.

## Key message
Get faster, more credible performance insight without losing analyst control.

## Offer or CTA
Register for a product webinar with a benchmark checklist download.

## Channel plan
LinkedIn, email, partner newsletter, webinar landing page.

## Content assets needed
Landing page copy, email sequence, social posts, webinar outline.

## Risks and review checkpoints
Avoid unsupported automation claims and confirm benchmark language.

## KPIs
Registrations, MQLs, meeting rate, influenced pipeline.

## Recommended next actions
Finalize positioning, draft webinar assets, set experiment thresholds.
