# Experiment Plan Example

## Objective
Improve CTR on LinkedIn launch ads.

## Hypothesis
Outcome-led messaging will outperform feature-led messaging.

## Test design
A/B test two creative concepts with equal budget split.

## Audience and channel
Mid-market SaaS marketers on LinkedIn.

## Variants
Variant A: time-to-insight message. Variant B: analyst-control message.

## Metrics and thresholds
Primary metric CTR, secondary metric landing page CVR, success threshold +15% CTR.

## Risks and confounders
Audience overlap, creative fatigue, landing page mismatch.

## Decision rule
Promote winner only if CTR improves and downstream CVR does not degrade.
