# Email Sequence Plan Example

## Objective
Increase webinar registrations from engaged mid-market SaaS marketers.

## Audience
Existing leads who engaged with reporting and analytics content in the past 90 days.

## Sequence strategy
Three-email sequence: invite, proof/urgency follow-up, final reminder.

## Email breakdown
Email 1: webinar invitation.
Email 2: benchmark and credibility angle.
Email 3: final reminder and urgency.

## CTA recommendations
Register now, save your seat.

## Personalization notes
Reference prior analytics or reporting engagement when available.

## Measurement plan
Open rate, CTR, registration rate, conversion by segment.

## Risks and review checkpoints
Avoid inflated claims, keep urgency credible, align with webinar landing page copy.
