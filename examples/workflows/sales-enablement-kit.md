# Sales Enablement Kit Example

## Objective
Equip account executives to position the new AI reporting feature credibly.

## Audience or seller context
AE team selling to VP Marketing and marketing ops leaders.

## Core message
The feature helps teams get to credible insight faster without removing analyst oversight.

## Proof points
Faster reporting workflow, benchmark insights, improved stakeholder readiness.

## Objection handling
Address trust, integration complexity, and AI overclaim skepticism.

## Talk tracks
Pain-led opener, trust-oriented explanation, demo transition language.

## Asset recommendations
One-pager, objection-handling sheet, demo checklist.

## Enablement next steps
Enablement session, talk-track practice, and feedback loop from first calls.
