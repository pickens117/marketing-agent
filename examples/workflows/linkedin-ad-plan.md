# LinkedIn Ad Plan Example

## Objective
Drive demo requests from mid-market B2B SaaS marketing leaders.

## Audience
VP Marketing, Demand Gen leaders, and marketing ops leaders at 100-1000 employee SaaS companies.

## Offer
Live demo plus benchmark checklist download.

## Creative angles
Time-to-insight, analyst control, and leadership-ready reporting.

## Ad copy variants
Variant 1: outcome-led pain angle.
Variant 2: analyst-control credibility angle.
Variant 3: benchmark/reporting efficiency angle.

## CTA recommendations
Book a demo, register for webinar, download checklist.

## Measurement plan
CTR, landing page CVR, demo request rate, influenced pipeline.

## Risks and review checkpoints
Avoid unsupported automation claims and overpromising on reporting outcomes.
