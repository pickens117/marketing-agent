# Meta Ad Plan Example

## Objective
Generate awareness and leads for an AI reporting feature launch.

## Audience hypotheses
SaaS marketers interested in analytics, attribution, and reporting ops; lookalikes of qualified leads; retargeting of engaged site visitors.

## Offer
Checklist download and webinar signup.

## Creative angles
Before/after reporting pain, workflow simplification, and proof-of-control messaging.

## Ad copy variants
Variant 1: pain-led hook.
Variant 2: curiosity-led benchmark hook.
Variant 3: control-and-credibility hook.

## CTA recommendations
Download now, learn more, register.

## Measurement plan
CTR, CPL, landing page CVR, lead quality, frequency.

## Risks and review checkpoints
Watch for vague AI promises, low-signal audience targeting, and creative fatigue.
